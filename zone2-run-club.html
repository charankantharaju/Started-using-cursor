About ZONE 2

More than just a running club - we're a community

Our Story

Founded in 2018, ZONE 2 started as a small group of friends who loved running together. Today, we've grown into a thriving community of over 500 runners of all levels, united by our passion for the sport and our commitment to supporting each other's goals.

Meet Our Coach

CHARAN K S

Head Coach & Founder

Certified Running Coach Sports Science Graduate 10+ Years Experience

CHARAN K S brings over a decade of running expertise to ZONE 2. As a certified running coach and sports science graduate, he has helped hundreds of runners achieve their goals, from first-time 5K finishers to Boston Marathon qualifiers.
